02 Getting here +


  • We're quite easy to get to, being located not far from the M56 or Chester city centre.

  • Whether you're travelling here by bus, train, car or bike, Chester Zoo is easy to get to with transport links from Chester, Liverpool & Manchester.

  • Find us on Google Maps.
03 Find your way around +

  • With 128 acres to explore, no two visits are the same. Use our app to personalise your day and find the best routes for you.

  • Search for your favourite animal, get directions and check out the latest daily programmes to make the most of your day with us.

04 Car parking +

  • Car parking on site is free for cars and coaches. For electric vehicles, we also have 26 x 7kw electric car charging points. We charge per kwh, and up to date costs can be found on the Pod Point app.

  • By charging your car at the zoo you’ll be helping us to invest in more charging points in the future and to reach our ambitions of being an even more sustainable zoo.

05 Accessibility +

  • We want all of our visitors to have an amazing day at the zoo, and we’re working hard to be as accessible as possible.

  • We currently offer free admission for carers, and free mobility scooter hire, as well as sensory guides and BSL signage at several locations across the zoo. Take a look at our accessibility page for more information.
